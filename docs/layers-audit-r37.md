## Executive Summary

HexDek implements a **substantial, well-tested subset** of MTG's continuous effects (¬ß613), replacement (¬ß614), and prevention (¬ß615) systems. The engine handles the **canonical layer paradoxes** (Humility + Opalescence, Blood Moon + Dryad Arbor, Mycosynth + Painter) correctly and includes sophisticated **dependency ordering** per ¬ß613.8. However, several layers and special cases remain unimplemented:

- **Layer 1b (face-down):** Stub only; no morph/manifest/disguise handlers
- **Layer 2 (control-changing):** Infrastructure present but no card handlers
- **Layer 7a (characteristic-defining abilities):** Called but empty; no cards like Tarmogoyf
- Replacement/prevention: 17+ effects implemented; no triggers-replaced-by yet

## Layer 1: Copy Effects (¬ß613.1a)

### Status: **PARTIAL**

**Primary handler:** `CopyPermanentLayered()` at `internal/gameengine/layers.go:860-926`

### Implementation
- Snapshot copiable characteristics at copy time per ¬ß706.2
- Reads from source's printed Card baseline, not layer-modified values
